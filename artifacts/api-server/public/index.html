The Challenge

The Hidden Inequality in Education

Millions of visually impaired and writing-disabled students across India rely on human scribes to complete homework and take exams — a system that is fragile, inconsistent, and compromises dignity.

Privacy Compromise

Students must dictate answers to a third party, undermining academic integrity and emotional privacy.

Unequal Exam Speed

Scribe-dependent students are at the mercy of another person's pace, comprehension, and availability.

Emotional Dependency

Constant reliance on others erodes self-confidence and academic autonomy over time.

Administrative Burden

Institutions must recruit, train, and schedule scribes for every examination — costly and unreliable.

No Independent Homework

Without a scribe, students cannot complete written assignments independently at home.

Compliance Gaps

Many institutions lack documented inclusion practices, creating risk during NAAC audits.

The Solution

From Dependency
to Self-Reliance

Enabelo Apps replaces the scribe with AI-powered voice technology. Students listen to questions, speak their answers, and the system converts speech into structured text — no intermediaries, no compromise.

About

About Enabelo Apps

A pioneering accessible educational platform for Visually Impaired and Writing-Disabled Students — built in India, for India and the world.

Our Mission

Enabelo Apps was created from a simple but powerful belief: every student deserves the right to express their knowledge independently. For millions of visually impaired and writing-disabled students in India, that right has long been denied by the scribe system.

We replace dependency with technology — giving students their voice, their dignity, and their academic freedom.
